Paddy

Wheat

Best techniques

Wheat

  1. 1 gallon of 28-0-0 provides approximately 3 lbs of N
  2. MAP is often preferred over DAP as a pop-up (placement with the seed) fertilizer
  3. Polyphosphates have the chemical formula H4P2O10. A fertilizer that contains polyphosphates is 10-34-0.

Maize

Best techniques

Maize

  1. Apply quarter of the dose of N; full dose of P2O and K2O basally before sowing.
  2. Apply the fertilizer mixture along the furrows evenly and cover to a depth of 4 cm with soil.
  3. If bed system of planting is followed, open furrows 6 cm deep at a distance of 60 cm apart.
